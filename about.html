About Me

Gina Lyn Cox is a certified Dispensary Associate accredited by the Green Flower Institute. Her Credly certification is a testament to her extensive knowledge of cannabis products, laws, and customer service standards. She is also an Independent Distributor with CannaGlobe, a leading supplier of wellness Hemp-Derived products. Currently, Gina is further enhancing her cannabis expertise as a Ganjier Student. Passionate about education and making a meaningful impact, she is in the process of setting up a cannabis-focused YouTube channel where she will cover a wide range of topics, from product reviews to in-depth interviews. Her goal is to break down stigmas, foster a community of informed consumers, and contribute positively to the industry.
